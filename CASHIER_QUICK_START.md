## â“ Common Questions

**Q: What if I pick the wrong size?**
A: Just click a different chip! The selection changes instantly.

**Q: Can I add multiple sizes of the same product?**
A: Yes! Search again, click different chip, and add. They'll be separate items in the cart.

A: That product has no variants. Just click Add like before!

**Q: Why do some products show chips and others don't?**
